we

At we_magazine we believe that the Internet has the power to change the world for the better. Our connectedness is re-writing the way we work, live, produce and consume things and act as a society. And it’s also re-writing the way we drive change – as we have seen and still see it in various fields all around the world.


the arts

Artists have always been at the forefront of cultural innovation and cultural change. They embraced the Internet in its early days and created new art forms around it. Art has expanded not only in the virtual world, it also expanded – with the help of digital technologies – into a diverse range of new disciplines such as biology, physics, astronomy, social sciences among them.


we and the arts

… is trying to connect the we and the arts by showing art pieces from all over the world rooted in various disciplines. Whenever we could we linked our stories to the art of Oubey, the Mindkiss project, because his art


  • celebrates the complexity and diversity in arts (including physics, astrophysics, compelxity research, biology)
  • is an attempt to visulize collective intelligence
  • symbolizes everything is connected with everything > NETWORK
  • resists “traditional” structures in the art scene
  • was made to create something bigger. Art as one way to achieve a common understanding of what a greater WE could be
